## Confirmation bias  #â³

Confirmation bias is the tendency to search for, interpret, favor, and recall information in a way that confirms or supports one's [subjective bias](Subjective%20bias.md)es.Â It is do to the fact that [our brains unconsciously focus on only a select few of the sensory signals it receives that it considers important](Our%20brains%20unconsciously%20focus%20on%20only%20a%20select%20few%20of%20the%20sensory%20signals%20it%20receives%20that%20it%20considers%20important.md). People display this bias when they select information that supports their views, ignoring contrary information, or when they interpret ambiguous evidence as supporting their existing attitudes.
