## Right Action  #⏳

Right Action is a division of [The Nobel Eightfold Path](The%20Nobel%20Eightfold%20Path.md). It involves abstaining from:

* killing
* stealing
* sexual misconduct

##### References

* Saṃyutta Nikāya 45:8; V 8–10
