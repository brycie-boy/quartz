## Neurotransmitter  #🧠 

A neurotransmitter is a signaling molecule secreted by a [[neuron]] to affect another [[cell]] across a [[synapse]]. The [[cell]] receiving the signal may be another [[neuron]] but could also be a gland or muscle [[cell]].
