## The third jhana  #⏳ 

The third of [[the jhanas]] involves of the fading away of the [[piti]] from [[the first jhana]] and [[the second jhana]] from which comes the feeling of [[sukha]].

___

- Saṃyutta Nikāya 45:8; V 8–10
