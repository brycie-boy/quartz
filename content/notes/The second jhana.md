## The second jhana  #⏳ 

The second of [[the jhanas]] involves internal confidence, the establishment of [[flow]], the subsiding of the thought and examination of [[the first jhana]], and includes [[piti]] born from concentration.

___

##### References

- Saṃyutta Nikāya 45:8; V 8–10
