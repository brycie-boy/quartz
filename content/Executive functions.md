## Executive functions # 

Executive functions are a set of of cognitive processes that are necessary for cognitive control of behavior: selecting and successfully monitoring behaviors that facilitate the attainment of chosen goals. The [[prefrontal cortex]] plays a large role in executive functions. Executive functions include basic cognitive processes such as:

- [[attention control]]
- [[cognitive inhibition]]
- [[inhibitory control]]
- [[working memory]]
- [[cognitive flexibility]]
