## The seven factors of enlightenment  #⏳ 

The seven factors of enlightenment consist of: 

- [[mindfulness]]
- Investigation
- diligence
- [[piti]]
- tranquility
- concentration
- equanimity

___

##### References

- (Samyutta Nikaya 54:13; V 328–33 ≠ Majjhima Nikaya 118.15–43; III 82–88)
