## Right Speech  #⏳

Right Speech is a division of [The Nobel Eightfold Path](The%20Nobel%20Eightfold%20Path.md). It involves abstaining from:

* lying
* malicous speech
* harsh speech
* idle chatter

---

* Saṃyutta Nikāya 45:8; V 8–10
