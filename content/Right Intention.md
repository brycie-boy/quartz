## Right Intention  #⏳

Right intention is a division of [The Nobel Eightfold Path](The%20Nobel%20Eightfold%20Path.md). It involves the intention of:

* renunciation
* good will
* harmlessness

---

##### References

* Saṃyutta Nikāya 45:8; V 8–10
