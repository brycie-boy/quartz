## consummate #⏳

*adjective*

1. extremely skilled or accomplished 
   "a *consummate* professional"

1. of the highest degree
   "*consummate* skill"

1. complete in every detail : perfect
   "a *consummate* model of a car"

*verb*

1. finish, complete
   "*consummate* a business deal"

1. to make perfect
