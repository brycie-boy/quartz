## consummate #⏳ 

_adjective_

1. extremely skilled or accomplished 
   "a _consummate_ professional"

2. of the highest degree
   "_consummate_ skill"

3. complete in every detail : perfect
   "a _consummate_ model of a car"

_verb_

1. finish, complete
   "_consummate_ a business deal"

2. to make perfect
