## Left hemisphere

The left brain [brain hemisphere]() is responsible for [spotlight consciousness](Spotlight%20consciousness.md). An animal needs to be able to focus their attention narrowly and with precision, kind of like a spotlight. For example, a bird needs to focus on a grain of corn that it must eat, in order to pick it out from, say, the pieces of grit on which it lies.
